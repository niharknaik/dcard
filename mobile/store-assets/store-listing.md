## Full description (max 4000 characters)

DCard — your digital visiting card, everywhere.

Ditch paper business cards. With DCard you can create a beautiful digital visiting card in minutes, share it with a single tap or QR code, capture leads, and see who's viewing your card — all from your phone.

Whether you're a freelancer, founder, sales professional, consultant, or growing a small business, DCard helps you make a memorable first impression and never miss a connection.

WHY DCARD
• Create a stunning card — add your name, photo, role, company, contact details and bio.
• Share instantly — send your card by link or let anyone scan your personal QR code.
• Capture leads — visitors leave their details right from your public card.
• Track performance — see card views and lead activity with built-in analytics.
• Show your work — add a portfolio, services and social links.

FEATURES
• Beautiful templates — choose from a marketplace of free and premium designs.
• Personal QR code — perfect for events, meetings and networking.
• Lead capture & inbox — collect and manage enquiries in one place.
• Analytics — understand how your card is performing.
• Portfolio & services — highlight exactly what you offer.
• Social links — connect every profile in one tap.
• Rewards & referrals — earn points and invite others to DCard.
• Teams (Business) — manage cards and view analytics across your team.

WHO IT'S FOR
• Freelancers and creators who want a professional presence.
• Founders and small businesses building their brand.
• Sales, real estate and consulting professionals who network often.
• Anyone tired of printing and reprinting paper cards.

PLANS
DCard is free to get started. Upgrade to Premium or Business to unlock advanced features, premium templates and team tools. Subscriptions and some template unlocks are optional in-app purchases. The app also contains ads.

PRIVACY & TRUST
Your data stays yours. DCard uses secure, encrypted connections, gives you full control of your information, and lets you export or delete your account at any time from the app. DCard is intended for users aged 18 and over.

Make every introduction count. Download DCard and share your card with the world.
